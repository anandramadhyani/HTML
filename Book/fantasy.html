  1. HARRY POTTER SERIES

    J.K. ROWLINGS

    Synopsis:

    The story follows the life of young orphan Harry Potter, who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he makes friends and enemies, learns magic, and uncovers the truth about his family and the dark wizard who killed his parents. Over the course of the series, Harry faces numerous challenges, including battles against the dark wizard Voldemort and his followers, as he seeks to protect the wizarding world and those he loves. The story is filled with themes of love, friendship, sacrifice, and the power of good over evil.

  2. HUNGER GAMES SERIES
    SUZANNE COLLINS

    Synopsis:

    Divided into twelve districts and ruled by the oppressive Capitol, the series revolves around the life of Katniss Everdeen. When Katniss volunteers to participate in the annual Hunger Games to save her sister, she becomes an unexpected symbol of rebellion against the Capitol's tyranny.
    Throughout the trilogy, themes of social inequality, government control, personal sacrifice, and the power of resilience take center stage. The narrative explores Katniss's evolution from a reluctant fighter to a reluctant symbol of hope and defiance. The series combines intense action, emotional depth, and ethical dilemmas, culminating in a battle for justice and freedom that challenges the very foundations of Panem's society.

    You can buy this from Amazon.

  3. LORD OF THE RINGS SERIES

    J.R.R. TOLKIEN

    Synopsis:

    The trilogy follows Frodo Baggins and his companions as they embark on a perilous journey to destroy the One Ring, a powerful artifact that can bring destruction if it falls into the wrong hands.
    Throughout their quest, the characters encounter diverse landscapes, face daunting challenges, and forge unbreakable bonds. Themes of friendship, bravery, and the battle against darkness run through the narrative.
