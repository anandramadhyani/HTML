  1. SHIVA TRILOGY

    AMISH TRIPATHI

    Synopsis:

    The series is set in an ancient Indian kingdom and follows the story of a tribal leader named Shiva who discovers that he is the prophesied savior of the kingdom and must fight against the evil Nagas to save his people. Along the way, Shiva discovers secrets about his past, learns about the power of good and evil and ultimately becomes a god-like figure in the eyes of his people.

  2. KALKI TRILOGY

    KEVIN MISSAL

    Synopsis:

    The narrative follows Kalki's journey as he embraces his destiny to restore righteousness in a corrupt world. Along the way, he confronts challenges, uncovers hidden truths, and battles dark forces, embodying themes of dharma, destiny, and the timeless struggle between good and evil. The series creatively weaves mythology, adventure, and philosophy to offer readers a contemporary exploration of ancient prophecies and the power of a heroic figure to bring about change.

  3. PALACE OF ILLUSIONS

    CHITRA BANERJEE DIVAKARUNI

    Synopsis:

    The Palace of Illusions traces the princess Panchaali's life, beginning with her birth in fire and following her spirited balancing act as a woman with five husbands who have been cheated out of their father's kingdom. Panchaali is swept into their quest to reclaim their birthright, remaining at their side through years of exile and a terrible civil war involving all the important kings of India. Meanwhile, we never lose sight of her strategic duels with her mother-in-law, her complicated friendship with the enigmatic Krishna, or her secret attraction to the mysterious man who is her husbands' most dangerous enemy. Panchaali is a fiery female redefining for us a world of warriors, gods, and the ever-manipulating hands of fate.

    You can buy this from Amazon.
